# Business Questions This Project Answers

## Revenue
- What is total GMV (Gross Merchandise Value) by month/quarter/year?
- Which months show the highest and lowest revenue?

## Customer Behavior  
- How many unique customers do we have?
- What % of customers are repeat buyers?
- What is the average order value?

## Product Performance
- Which product categories generate the most revenue?
- Which categories have the most orders vs. highest revenue per order?

## Seller Performance
- Which sellers generate the most revenue?
- Which sellers have the worst delivery times?

## Delivery & Logistics
- What is the average delivery time (ordered → delivered)?
- What % of orders are delivered late vs. on time?
- Which states have the worst delivery performance?

## Customer Satisfaction
- What is the average review score overall?
- Is there a correlation between delivery delay and review score?

## Payment Behavior
- What payment methods do customers prefer?
- How many installments do customers typically use?

## Geography
- Which Brazilian states generate the most revenue?
- Where are our sellers concentrated vs. customers?
